Delete Your Account

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

Premier University Central allows users to permanently delete their account directly from within the application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Premier University Central.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Enter your email address and password to re-authenticate your account.
  6. Tap Delete Account to permanently delete your account.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information is permanently deleted where applicable:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account.
  • Your profile information.
  • Your email address.
  • Your username.
  • Your profile picture.
  • Your private messages associated with your account.
  • Your blocked users list.
  • Your notification records.

Data That May Be Retained

To maintain the integrity and safety of the university community, certain information may be retained after account deletion where permitted by applicable law, including:

  • Posts and comments that have been reported for moderation.
  • Information required to comply with legal obligations.

Any retained information is kept only for as long as necessary for moderation, legal, or security purposes.
